COURSE INFORMATION

Pharmaceutical Sciences

Pharmaceutical Sciences focuses on drug discovery, development, formulation, and safe, effective healthcare application.

Diploma in Radiology

Diploma in Radiology equips students with skills in medical imaging, diagnostics, and patient care techniques.

Diploma in Clinical Dentistry

Diploma in Clinical Dentistry trains students in oral health care, dental procedures, and patient management techniques.

Diploma in Clinical Medicine

Diploma in Clinical Medicine focuses on training healthcare professionals in diagnosing, treating, and managing common medical conditions.

Diploma in Social Work

Diploma in Social Work equips students with skills to support individuals, families, and communities in overcoming challenges.

Diploma in Community Development

The Diploma in Community Development equips you with the essential skills to create positive change, empower communities, and drive sustainable growth, preparing you for impactful roles in society

Diploma in Laboratory Assistant

Laboratory Assistant provides hands-on training in laboratory techniques, preparing you for a rewarding career in scientific research, healthcare, and quality control

Diploma in medical Laboratory

Medical Laboratory offers comprehensive training in diagnostic techniques, equipping you with the skills to support healthcare professionals and contribute to accurate medical diagnoses

Diploma in optometry

Optometry provides specialized knowledge and practical skills to diagnose and manage eye conditions, preparing you for a fulfilling career in vision care and eye health

Diploma in physiotherapy

The Diploma in Physiotherapy offers in-depth training in physical rehabilitation, equipping you with the skills to improve mobility, manage pain, and enhance the quality of life for patients

Information and comunication technology

Information and Communication Technology (ICT) provides essential skills in modern technology, preparing you for dynamic roles in tech development, network management, and digital innovation

Diploma in horticulture and agriculture production

The Diploma in Horticulture and Agriculture Production equips you with practical knowledge and skills in crop management, sustainable farming practices, and agricultural production, preparing you for a successful career in the growing agriculture sector

Diploma in haelth recod and information technology

The Diploma in Health Record and Information Technology combines healthcare management with IT skills, preparing you to efficiently manage patient data and support healthcare systems through innovative technology."

Diploma in Clinical Nutrition

The Diploma in Clinical Nutrition provides in-depth knowledge of nutrition science, enabling you to design effective dietary plans and promote health and well-being through personalized nutrition care.

Diploma in Environment Health Sciences

The Diploma in Environmental Health Sciences prepares you to tackle public health challenges by focusing on environmental factors that affect well-being, equipping you to promote healthier and safer communities
